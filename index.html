Discipline without rigidity.

Envelopes give structure. Categories give flexibility. You pick how rigorous each one needs to be — and change your mind whenever.

Two-mode categories

Use them as plain classifiers, or opt one into allocated mode when you want it to hold cash. Switch any time — your history stays put.

Credit cards

When you charge something, the money moves from your spending envelope into a reserve for that card. When the statement arrives, the cash is already set aside. Tracks your statement cycle, not the calendar month.

Goals with teeth

Three kinds: hit a target, hold a threshold, or contribute every period. Goals attach to any category — including the flexible ones.

Your money. Your device. End of story.

werkBudget is local-first. The only sync is your own iCloud private database — nobody else, including us, can see it.

  • No accounts to create; Sign in with Apple, optional Face ID lock
